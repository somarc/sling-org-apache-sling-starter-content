What is Biometric Authentication?

Use your device's Face ID, Touch ID, or Windows Hello to authenticate to Blockchain AEM. Your biometric data never leaves your device - only cryptographic signatures are sent.

How It Works

  1. Register: Link your Ethereum wallet to a biometric passkey
  2. Authenticate: Scan biometric to sign write proposals
  3. Publish: Content is written to oak-chain with Face ID signature
